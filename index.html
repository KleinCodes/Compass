Compass++ Explore Confidently

Compass++ is the perfect app for any explorer. Both experts and beginners alike will find features they love in a beautifully designed canvas. Compass++ includes a compass, of course, a display of your current altitude, a ruler for those emergencies and a map tucked below.

Simple to use

Open the app on your mobile phone, instantly view your direction and altitude.

View the Map

Slide down the main Compass to view a simple map where you can pan and zoom.
